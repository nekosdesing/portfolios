FAQ

How long does a commission take?

The standard turnaround time is between 3 to 7 days. Most orders are fully completed within a single week, though this can vary slightly based on the total number of designs requested.

Can I request revisions?

Yes. I will send you an initial sketch beforehand so you can review the layout and request any necessary adjustments. Once the sketch is approved and the final coloring phase begins, only minor adjustments can be made.

What will I receive upon completion?

You will receive the final high-quality assets in transparent PNG format, fully optimized and ready to upload to Twitch and Discord. Each emote is delivered in the three official required sizes:

  • 112 × 112 px
  • 56 × 56 px
  • 28 × 28 px

Are these designs exclusive?

Yes, absolutely. Every commission is drawn entirely from scratch for each individual client, ensuring it perfectly matches your character, branding, or personal style.

Can you design assets based on my character or avatar?

Of course! Feel free to provide reference images, character sheets, or a detailed description so I can bring your exact persona to life.
